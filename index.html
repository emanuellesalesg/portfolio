About me

My name is Emanuelle Gonçalo, and I am a Bachelor’s degree student in Computer Science. I began my programming journey with the Java language. During my Electrical Engineering course, I had the opportunity to learn C and C++. I decided to switch fields because I find myself more aligned with the world of programming. I enjoy logical challenges and keeping website designs attractive. I have a knack for creating layouts and making them responsive.

I have a strong interest in the backend area, but my goal is to become a fullstack developer. Currently, I am pursuing a Bachelor’s degree in Computer Science, taking the Fullstack course at B7web, and participating in the Google Cloud Academy to explore the cloud world.

My main characteristics include a love for socializing and working in a team, enjoying planning my day, being proactive and creative, and liking to innovate. I believe that software development allows me to create and transcribe my ideas and solutions into code, something I consider a form of contemporary digital art.
